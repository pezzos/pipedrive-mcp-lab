# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Trusted client users allowed through Cloudflare Access. They use Claude to work
with a shared Pipedrive account and visit the settings page only to understand
or change their own MCP capabilities.

## Product Purpose

Provide a dependable remote Pipedrive MCP that connects once, refreshes access
without routine intervention, starts read-only, and makes every increase in
authority explicit. Success means users can understand their current access in
seconds and cannot change another user's permissions.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, reassuring. The interface states consequences plainly and avoids
drama, novelty, or technical theatre.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a decorative SaaS dashboard, a sales landing page, a dense
developer console, or an alarm-heavy security product. Avoid ornamental cards,
gratuitous metrics, gradients, glass effects, and ambiguous icon-only controls.

## Design Principles

1. Explain consequences before asking for confirmation.
2. Keep safe defaults visible and easy to restore.
3. Separate ordinary writes, deletion, and Mailbox access conceptually.
4. Show only the current user's authority and operational status.
5. Prefer plain language and reversible inline actions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast, focus, keyboard operation, labels, status
announcements, and error identification. Respect reduced-motion preferences and
never use color as the only signal.
